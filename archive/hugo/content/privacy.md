Leisure Oaks Park ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

### Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Reservation or service-related details you provide
- Communication preferences

### How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Manage reservations, bookings, and park services
- Send confirmations and updates
- Respond to inquiries
- Provide customer support
- Improve our services

### SMS Communications

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from Leisure Oaks Park:

- Message frequency may vary.
- Message and data rates may apply.
- You can opt out at any time by replying **STOP**.
- You can reply **HELP** for assistance.

We do **not** send marketing or promotional messages. All SMS communications are strictly service-related, such as reservation confirmations, check-in details, and park notifications.

### Information Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information only:

- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights or property
- With service providers necessary to deliver our services

### Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your personal information.
